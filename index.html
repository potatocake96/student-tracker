Cybersecure by Design

Your data is protected with industry-best practices.

Secure Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication, an industry-standard service, to handle sign-ins securely. Your credentials are never stored on our servers.

Data Encryption

All of your data is encrypted both in transit (between your device and our servers) and at rest, ensuring it remains private and protected.

Data Isolation

Using Firestore Security Rules, each teacher's and school's data is strictly isolated, preventing any possibility of unauthorized cross-account access.
